About Empowervision

Guiding South African businesses toward empowerment, compliance, and sustainable growth since 2007.

Our Story

Founded in Midrand, Gauteng, Empowervision Consulting Services was established to help businesses navigate the complexities of B-BBEE compliance and transformation. Over nearly two decades, we’ve partnered with organizations across industries, empowering them to unlock opportunities, strengthen their workforce, and achieve sustainable success.

Our Journey

2007

Empowervision Consulting Services is founded, focusing on B-BBEE compliance and empowerment strategies.

2012

Expanded services to include skills development programs, helping businesses invest in their workforce.

2015

Partnered with organizations across Gauteng, building a reputation for integrity and excellence.

2020

Introduced financial guidance services, supporting clients through regulatory and economic challenges.

2026

Celebrating nearly two decades of empowering businesses and communities across South Africa.
